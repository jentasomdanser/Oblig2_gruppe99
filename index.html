Games we look forward to see on October 20.

12 days ago, Nintendo of America announced that Super Mario Bros Wonder. will arrive on October 20 for Nintendo Switch.

Håvard Elgsaas, Yasmin Avalo, Emma Agnethe Rasch Eldby - 9/12/2023, 18:42 PM

 



Watch the colorful story and gameplay trailer for Super Mario Bros Wonder.

The characters we all have come to love will return in Super Mario Bros Wonder:

  • Mario
  • Luigi
  • Peach
  • Daisy
  • Yellow Toad
  • Blue Toad
  • Toadette
  • 4 different colored versions of Yoshi
  • Nabbit

    • New enemies will be introduced

  • Hoppycats, "who will jump when you jump."
  • Melon Piranha Plants, "who will spit seeds from their mouth."
  • Condarts. "They will fly at you and stick their beaks where they don't belong."
  • Konks. "They will even plow through goo."
  • Mumsies. "They can be unraveled if you position yourself just right."
  • Maw-Maws. "They will eat anything that comes to close to their gaping mouths."
  • Old fiends like Goombas, Koopas Troopas, Boos, Lakitus and many more!

    • New Powerups!

      Elephant form:
  • Use Marios New size to break through blocks and run over large gaps. Use Marios elephant trunk to spit water.
      • Bubble form:
  • Allows you to blow light, floating bubbles that can capture and defeat enemies, and will drift towards nearby foes. You can even "defeat typically impervious foes", and "knock out enemies on the other side of walls!". The bubbles can also be used as platforms for parkour.
      • Drill form:
  • You can easly defeat spiky or hard-headed enemies. You can drill down, drill down under ground and "even burrow into the ceiling!"

    • What we know about Wonder Flowers.

  • The power Bowser stole in the start of the game.
  • If you touch a Wonder Flower in a course, a wonder effect will trigger, with different outcomes for each and every flower.

    • Badges:

  • Change the way you play when equipped. Here are some examples:
  • Wall-Climb Jump
  • Parachute Cap: Glide in the air with use of your cap.
  • Dolphin Kick: Swim faster under water, and break underwater blocks.
  • Crouching High Jump: Jump higher after crouching.
  • Grappling Vine: Used as a grappling hock to easier get from point A to B.
  • Safety Bounce: Jump when you touch lava to not die, but instead make a jump.
  • Sensor: Find important items

    • New voice for Mario and Luigi?!



    Charles Martinet will no longer voice the voices for Mario and Luigi, and he will instead become a Mario Ambassador.
