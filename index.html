UFO spotted over OsloMet!

And is elvish a language that actually existed in elven time?

Knut Flapstrudel - 9/13/2023, 11:00 AM

 

Photographer: Yasmin N. Garcia Avalo

 

"Aliens, Elves, and Hovering Spaceships: Unearthed Tablet Reveals Mind-Boggling Cosmic Chronicles at OsloMet!"

In a bizarre turn of events, an otherworldly discovery has sent shockwaves through the hallowed halls of OsloMet University, shattering the boundaries of human understanding and challenging the very fabric of our reality.

Reports of a spaceship ominously hovering above the university's rooftop have surfaced, along with the unearthing of a mysterious tablet engraved with elvish scriptures recounting a cosmic war of epic proportions.

But before you don your tinfoil hat, let's dive into the details of this extraordinary tale.

The OsloMet Extraterrestrial Extravaganza

Picture this: a crisp morning at OsloMet University, with students diligently pouring over textbooks, and professors lost in the world of academia. Suddenly, whispers of an unusual sighting began to circulate like wildfire. Eyewitness accounts claim that a colossal spaceship appeared out of thin air, perching precariously atop the university's roof. Was it a prank? A stunt? Or something far more extraterrestrial?

The Elvish Enigma

As if the spaceship sighting weren't perplexing enough, a team of archaeologists, led by Professor Elinor Runekeeper, stumbled upon a buried treasure unlike any other. Deep within the OsloMet campus, they unearthed an ancient tablet adorned with intricate elvish scriptures. These enigmatic inscriptions tell of a colossal cosmic clash, pitting elves, dwarves, powerful wizards, humans, and even aliens against each other in an epic battle for supremacy. Just when you thought fantasy novels were stretching the limits of imagination!

The Material Mystery

But here's where the plot thickens. Experts in the field are baffled by the material of the tablet itself. It's composed of a substance that defies earthly logic, one that doesn't exist in our known periodic table. Does this defy the laws of physics as we know them? Or is it just a clever prank by some advanced cosplaying aliens with a penchant for Tolkein?

Are We the Puppets of Cosmic Puppeteers?

As skeptics raise their eyebrows and conspiracy theorists sharpen their pens, one question looms large: could this all be a grand hoax? It's certainly possible. After all, humans are known for their creative imagination, and Photoshop can make even the most skeptical minds second-guess reality. But before we dismiss this as mere fiction, let's consider an alternative perspective.

Invoking Critical Thinking

Could it be that authorities are keeping secrets from us? Could it be that aliens have been visiting Earth for centuries, leaving breadcrumbs of their existence in the form of stories and mysterious artifacts? Are we simply too afraid to confront the great mysteries of the cosmos and the depths of human consciousness?

As we grapple with the bewildering events at OsloMet, perhaps it's time to cultivate a healthy sense of skepticism, not just towards the bizarre, but also towards those who would keep us in the dark. After all, if the tablet and hovering spaceship turn out to be elaborate ruses, they have successfully piqued our curiosity about the unknown. And who knows, maybe one day, the secrets of the cosmos will be revealed, and we'll find ourselves on the edge of a new frontier of understanding, or at the very least, a fantastic sci-fi novel.

Until then, keep your eyes on the skies and your minds open, for the universe is a vast and mysterious place, and sometimes the truth is stranger than fiction.

Elinor Runekeeper have translated some of the text from the scrolls, here is the results:

Part of the Elvish Scrolls

Mey noyhk nu Akhw-u Saea, leik Phaonden, Jeouk yeh Krapientas Akhwu-as bajks yeh Trnubus nu Uvana.

Wehk Gvanto nu Ovilanus nu famr nubus dwark cvawk yeh Akhwu Vaharu, ahjr lepsigus Akhw-us mahiru Uvana-Trnubus, igsoj rsjako vjeru injasa podjaka.

Hidak fam prisj rsjakova mey yeh ex-sydamus, fuiska famr nubus gra tasjka qril yeh Famralush.

 

Translation:

"In time of war, the elves, dwarfs and humans fought against the people of space.

After 100 of thousands of our kind were killed at the battlefield, we finally fought back the Space People, and they have never returned.

Yet we fear their return in the future, when our kind no longer walk the Earth".
